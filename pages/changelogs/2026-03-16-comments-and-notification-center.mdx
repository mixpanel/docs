Collaborate on your data without leaving Mixpanel.

You can now start threaded conversations directly on any report or board. Mention teammates to bring them in, react with emojis, and resolve threads when you’re done.

Stay up to date with the new Notification Center. Get notified when someone mentions you or replies to your threads, with quick links to jump back into the conversation. You can also receive notifications via email or Slack.

**Why this matters**
Keep discussions tied to the data they’re about—so decisions happen faster, context isn’t lost, and your team spends less time switching between tools.

**Key features:**
- Threaded comments on all reports and boards
- @mentions with in-app and email notifications 
- Unread tracking with badge counts
- Control: enable or disable comments per report
