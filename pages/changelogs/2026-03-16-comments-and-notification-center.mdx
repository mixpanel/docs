You can now discuss data directly on reports and boards without leaving Mixpanel.

**Comments** let you start threaded conversations on any report or board. @mention teammates to loop them in, add emoji reactions, and resolve threads when the discussion is complete.

The **Notification Center** keeps you up to date on comment activity. You'll be notified when someone mentions you or replies to a thread you're in, with direct links to jump straight to the conversation. You can also set up integrations to get notifications via email, slack, or teams

Key capabilities:
- Threaded comments on all report types and boards
- @mentions with in-app and email notifications
- Emoji reactions and thread resolution
- Unread tracking with badge counts
- Report owners can enable/disable comments per report
