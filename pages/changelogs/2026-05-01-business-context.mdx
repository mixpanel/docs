We're launching Business Context — a way to teach your AI assistant about your organization and projects, so it answers grounded in how you actually run your business.

When an AI assistant connects to Mixpanel through MCP, it now reads markdown context you've written about your company and your projects before answering. Less back-and-forth defining terms, more answers that already understand what a "qualified user" means in your data.

**What's new:**
- **Organization-level context:** Capture company background, business model, north star metrics, and customer segments. Edit in **Settings → Org → Business Context**.
- **Project-level context:** Document a project's event taxonomy, naming conventions, key dashboards, and team norms. Edit in **Settings → Project → Business Context**.
- **Read & write via MCP:** AI assistants can pull context with `Get-Business-Context` and update it with `Update-Business-Context` — ask your AI to "remember that…" or "what do you know about this project?"
