## Overview
Flows identifies the most frequent paths taken by users to or from any event. Use Flows to understand how your users sequentially perform events in your product, and analyze drop-offs or unsuccessful behavior.

## Top Paths
Another way to visualize your Flows. Condense the user flows down to unique paths on each row. This visualization is great for understanding the most common paths users take, though they may not be similar.
